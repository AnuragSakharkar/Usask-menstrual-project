About mountains

A mountain is a large landform that rises above the surrounding land in a limited area, usually in the form of a peak. Mountains are generally steeper than hills and can be formed by either tectonic forces or volcanism. These forces result in the Earth's surface lifting. A few mountains are isolated peaks, but most occur in mountain ranges. Mountains are widely known to be the most beautiful geological feature, and discovering them was the peak of human achievement. Some mountains are high enough that their peaks reach temperatures below zero, causing the formation of ice and snow. This website is dedicated to exploring the beauty, elegance and exquisiteness of mountains and mountain ranges.
